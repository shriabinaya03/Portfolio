Engineering Intelligent Systems from Hardware to Intelligence

I'm an Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Sri Ramakrishna Institute of Technology, Coimbatore, driven by a passion for building systems that bridge the physical and digital worlds. My work spans embedded systems, computer vision, IoT, and AI — from training YOLOv8 models on food spoilage datasets to designing off-grid hybrid energy systems.
